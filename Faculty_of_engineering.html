ABOUT ENGINEERING

The Faculty of Engineering at Swaminarayan University, Kalol, is committed to producing technically skilled, industry-ready engineers through a curriculum built on practical application, industry interface, and innovation. From Diploma to Postgraduate programs, the faculty ensures that students gain the skills, tools, and mindset required to thrive in real-world engineering environments.

Our engineering programs provide hands -on training in modern laboratories, workshops and field projects. Students also master the core theoretical concepts, but also technical problems-composition, CAD/CAM software, electrical systems, coding and infrastructure design.

Project-based learning, internship, industrial search and technical training on the site is an important part of the academic process, which ensures student educators with job-taire skills.

Students associate with financial equipment, commercial software, digital platforms and entrepreneurial thinking that match modern requirements for industry and trade. Courses are taught by experienced faculty and industry experts, with new technologies, stability and focus on smart engineering solutions.

WHY SHOULD YOU CHOOSE ENGINEERING?

Engineering is the basis for technological innovation and development of infrastructure in all fields.

Our programs focus on preparing engineers who can solve real industrial problems with practical knowledge and equipment. Students are trained for careers in production, IT, construction, transport, energy, automation, design and counseling.

AI, IoT, green energy and robotics demand and developing technologies with high industrial requirements -Engineering provides exciting, lifetime learning and growth.
